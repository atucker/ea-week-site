About us

Harvard College Effective Altruism is a student group devoted to TAKE FROM WEBSITE. In particular, we are committed to the ideas that TAKE FROM WEBSITE.

We host a dozen public talks each year, from such luminaries as philosopher Peter Singer, polymath Steven Pinker, and Skype co-founder Jaan Tallinn. Each semester, we award competitive fellowships to about twenty students, who meet weekly with our speakers and other guests, develop perspectives on ethics and global challenges, and gain access to an international network of humanitarians. HCEA also runs reading groups on more specific topics and maintains a blog.
