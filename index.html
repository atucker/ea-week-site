What’s your plan to change the world?

Your career = 80,000 hours of work, each of which is an opportunity to make the world a better place.

  • Will you reduce the global burden of disease?
  • Address international economic inequities?
  • Promote our planet’s resilience?
  • Leverage increasing interconnectivity to bridge social disconnects?

This is an incredible chance we have—to view our careers not only as occupations, but also as demonstrations of our values and visions for the world.

What are your values? What is your vision?

Let’s translate intentions to outcomes

It’s not enough for us to care—real change requires precise planning and concrete action. We must prioritize global challenges, learn from insights in research and public policy, and take directed actions whose impacts we can anticipate and understand.

We must make our altruism effective.

How can we each choose careers that will position us to realize our potential to improve the world?

Start with EA Week

Effective Altruism Week is a time to reflect on our values, explore global issues, and rethink our own life paths.

So, do you want to change the world? See you soon.
